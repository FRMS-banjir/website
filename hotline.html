Hotline Information

For immediate assistance during flood emergencies, you can reach out to the following hotlines:


  • National Disaster Management Agency: 03-8870 4800
  • Flood Relief Emergency Contact: 03-26871496/03-26871497

These hotlines are available 24/7 to assist with flood-related emergencies, rescue operations, and relief efforts. Please do not hesitate to call if you or your loved ones are in need of immediate assistance.
